OBSERVATION ON IMPROVEMENT¶

1. Volume is the base of Big Data, and the more the merrier. There were 60 outliers in the Funding section which had to be dropped.¶

2. I would suggest to follow a single unit of measurement for currency, as it would yield better results.¶

3. It is crucial that data is captured at a fast pace as it helps yield significant results. Stale data will give results that may not be useful in future.¶

4. Variety must also be taken care of as it is better to capture anything and everything of value in the business ecosystem. Data that look like it has no immediate value, can yield great results when processed correctly.¶

5. Veracity, or Trustworthiness of data holds utmost importance as without it models built on the data will not be of true value ¶

6. Value can be deemed as the most important aspect. If the Big Data system cannot derive value out of the whole exercise it isn’t worth involving it in the business.¶
